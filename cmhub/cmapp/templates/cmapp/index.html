Conversation

Likes

Likes are a great way to show you appreciate or support a Tweet. Tweets you liked are listed on the likes tab of your profile. They can also appear in the feeds of some subscribers.

Retweets

Retweets allow you to share Tweets that you think will interest your audience.They are also great for sharing user-generated content. Use the Quote Tweet option to add a touch of humor, amplify a reaction,

Comments

Comments to a Tweet are a very useful way to have personal conversations with your customers. They can also help create a buzz. An effective response is likely to generate many qualified engagements and subscribers.

Advice

Engage your audience on Twitter

To build your community on Twitter, it helps to identify and encourage connection opportunities.
Here are some suggestions:
Ask questions or use the Twitter Questions feature to start the conversation. When it makes sense, use GIFs, emojis, memes, or Tweets that contain only text to humanize communication and encourage interactions.
If you want your readers to take a specific action, say so (example: “Retweet if you can't wait for our new product!”). Stay on top of trends and opportunities to participate in conversations. Use Twitter chats to regularly interact with a group on a given topic.
